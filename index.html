About

I am passionate about physics and philosophy. This website is an invitation to have a look at my work. I hope it conveys my excitement about some of the pretty cool ideas that I work on together with my colleagues.
Regarding physics, I am mostly interested in quantum theory. I would like to find new ways to measure very small quantities by employing quantum systems. This field is also called quantum metrology and describes the art of measurements with non-classical systems.
There are questions beyond physics, that fall into the realm of philosophy. The natural sciences are in a way descriptive of the world, but they cannot make a normative assessment of their findings without appealing to philosophy.
One very recent example is the current progress in artificial intelligence. Computer science may develop breakthrough technologies, but that does not make them inherently good nor bad. It needs careful philosophical consideration to answer these questions. I work on these issues of artificial morality and ethical AI.
Feel free to get in touch for questions or if you are interested in a collaboration. I am always looking forward to meet new people and bridge the gap between society and the scientific community.
